This privacy policy aims to inform you about how we collect, use and protect your personal data when you visit our all-you-can-eat buffet restaurant. Please take the time to read this policy carefully to understand how we process your personal data.

d/ Customer satisfaction surveys: In order to continually improve our services, we may ask you to participate in customer satisfaction surveys. Your participation in these surveys is optional.

b. Legal Obligations: Under certain circumstances, we may be required to disclose your personal information to comply with laws, regulations, legal process or governmental requests.

We take the security of your personal information very seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure or alteration.
